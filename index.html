Secret Combo for Happiness – Ice cream & Friends

Ice cream and friends do happen in our lives. They both don’t need any special occasion or reason to celebrate. Both bring in Joy, Fun, Happiness, Smiles and Laughter. May it be a midnight cravings, reunions, parties, get-togethers, sleepovers, joint studies, exam results, love or breakups – friends and Ice creams play a subtle bonding.

With all the madness going on around you and your friends, ice creams add up to that much needed flavoured punch. It is so fun to remember the fights with friends to get those bigger chunks of ice cream scoop or secretly grabbing your friend’s cone once you have finished yours or licking that last bit of cream from your bowls, ice creams and friendship remain the fond memories to our generations.
Let’s recreate that magical ice cream memories with your friends at Chill’d Monk Ice Cream parlour or order at your doorsteps.

CHILL'D MONK - Company

It all began in 1998 when Chill'd Monk started as a unit of Scupy Ice cream, catering to basic ice cream needs. Considering the consumer's love for high quality, we initiated to serve you with a variety of flavours made with 100% milk-based ice cream to entice your taste buds. Procuring pure milk directly from our farmers and processing the ice cream with natural and honest ingredients has been our prime USP.

We are an ISO 9001:2015 certified company, taking pride in producing super-premium quality ice creams – the ones you won’t resist to try! With great passion, we at Chill’d monk serve exotic fruit-based ice creams like Pine Orange, Lychee, Jack Fruit, Tender Coconut, Rose Gulkand. Do experience our seasonal fruit ice creams made from natural fruits with a dash of newness.

Why wait? Just walk into our Customer Service outlets in Bangalore or order online now and get mesmerized with our Chill’d monk super quality and tasty ice cream. Happiness is rest assured!
